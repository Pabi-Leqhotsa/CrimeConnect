Safety Tips & Guidelines

Home Security

  • Always lock doors and windows
  • Use timer lights when away
  • Get to know your neighbors
  • Install security cameras

Personal Safety

  • Stay aware of surroundings
  • Walk in well-lit areas
  • Keep valuables hidden
  • Trust your instincts

Vehicle Safety

  • Lock car doors always
  • Park in well-lit areas
  • Hide valuables from view
  • Check backseat before entering

Digital Safety

  • Share location with trusted contacts
  • Keep phone charged
  • Save emergency contacts
  • Use safety apps

Emergency Procedures

In Case of Emergency:

  1. 1 Stay Calm - Take deep breaths and assess the situation
  2. 2 Call for Help - Dial emergency services immediately
  3. 3 Move to Safety - Get to a secure location if possible
  4. 4 Alert Community - Use the emergency button in the app
  5. 5 Provide Details - Share location and situation details

If You Witness a Crime:

  1. 1 Do not approach or intervene directly
  2. 2 Note physical descriptions and vehicle details
  3. 3 Remember time and location accurately
  4. 4 Call police immediately
  5. 5 Report through CrimeConnect app
